Curator orientation

How the annex works

ACQUIRE

Let the committee draw three artifacts no honest scholar would shelve together. Objections are raised, provenance is searched, strangers are certified.

CONNECT

Write the wall text. The conviction that was needless in the main building is mandatory here — the objects will not connect themselves.

CONFESS

3,000 words minimum, drafted at the curator’s desk. Surprise yourself before you attempt to surprise the reader. If you already see the link, deaccession.

Basement level · The acquisitions committee

Three artifacts, no acquaintance

The committee pulls live candidates from English Wikipedia, rejects the living, the political, the current, and the under-documented, then runs a provenance search between every surviving pair. Anything within reach of anything else — a shared category, a link path shorter than the registrar’s standard — is sent back to storage.
